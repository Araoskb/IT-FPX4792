ARConnect: A Military/Veteran Social Platform

ARConnect is a new and experimental social media website for those who have had experience/served in the military, have a passion for the military, or are in a Veteran status. When scrolling through other social media platforms like Facebook, you see many people that reply to certain posts that do not specifically have a clue as to what it is like to serve nor understand the "lingo" that many of us military folks pick up throughout our time in service. This platform aims to connect those who want to share experiences (fun or not) to others, whether for educational or recreational purposes. ARConnect hopes to create bonds between individuals that can change their lives to create the feel and sense of community not only across the United States, but the world.
